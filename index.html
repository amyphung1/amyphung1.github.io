Emergentech: Hack ASU

Emergentech is ASU's university-wide hackathon; a 3-day long on-campus event for engineering, business, and design students to channel their creativity and design thinking through technology.

I worked with The Luminosity Lab at Arizona State University and assisted with the organization of ASU's first annual Emergentech Hackathon. I executed several different marketing strategies by creating advertising content using Canva, managing the event's social media accounts (Twitter @emergentechASU and Instagram @emergentech), and sending out e-mail blasts to different student organizations and clubs on campus.

  • Date: March 2017
  • Client: Emergentech: Hack ASU
  • Category: Marketing

Social Media Marketing Internship

Admix Social is an online marketing firm located in Scottsdale, Arizona that develops customized strategies for advertisting, sales, and marketing.

I was a Social Media Marketing Intern at Admix Social for 5 months, running social media accounts of several different clients and executing different marketing strategies based on their core requirements and motivations. I coordinated the development of content for social media platforms such as: Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Twitter, and Instagram. I was required to familiarize myself with WordPress, SEO, and Hootsuite, as those were some of the applications and skills we primarily used for our clients for activities such as blogging.
