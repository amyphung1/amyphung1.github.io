Biography

Emergentech: Hack ASU

Emergentech is ASU's university-wide hackathon, which is a 3-day long on-campus event for engineering, business, and design students.

I was able to work with The Luminosity Lab at Arizona State University and assist with the organization of their first annual Emergentech Hackathon. I executed several different marketing strategies by creating content using Canva and managing their social media accounts: Twitter @emergentechASU and Instagram @emergentech, and sending out e-mail blasts to different student organizations on ASU's campus.

  • Date: March 2017
  • Client: Emergentech: Hack ASU
  • Category: Marketing
